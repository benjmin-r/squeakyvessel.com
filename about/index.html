My name is Benjamin Reitzammer.

Currently I'm a Ruby/Rails developer at crispymtn, working on Keyline.

Before that I was CTO for vaamo where I built the team and technology for the first four years of the company, so questions about engineering management, team building and fostering a great culture are more than welcome. All in all I have 15+ years of experience across various companies and teams.

Outside of work I spend most of my time with my family, the wonderfullest wife in the world and two awesome kids.
