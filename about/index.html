That I like people also makes my job as an Software Development Manager so crazily enjoyable.

Simple as that. My fav coffee place is Paul's Kaffeekommune in Mainz. If you've got at least some little time to spare and are remotely in that area, then you owe yourself a visit to this awesome place.

… Dotfiles

It's a bit silly, how happy those simple files with arcane symbols make me. But they give me quite some peace of mind, as they allow me to setup a productive working environment on almost any computer that I get access to and therefore free me of worrying about my computer breaking.
