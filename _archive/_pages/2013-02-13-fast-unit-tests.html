This training can be arranged as a 1-day or 2-day course. It will teach you basics of unit testing as well as advanced topics, so you get the most out of this very important technique which seems easy on the outside but can be hard to do well and integrate into your daily programming workflow.

The course is extremely hands-on and will get you out of your comfort-zone quickly.

Already sold? Then schedule a session now.

Audience

  • You know what a unit test is and use them sometimes but it always feels like they slow you down.
  • Your unit tests are somehow too complicated and you think there must be a better way.
  • You sometimes fix bugs more than once.
  • You heard that everybody should write tests for their code, but haven't really come around to trying it for yourself.

What you'll learn

  • How to write small and fast unit tests.
  • How to make writing unit tests a habit.
  • You'll learn to fix bugs while driving down costs for adding new features.
  • Keep your unit test suite fast and manageable even in large systems.

What you'll do

  • You'll work in your preferred programming language and environment. Examples will be in Java only, but easy to understand whatever your preferred environment is.
  • This course is very hands-on and you'll be programming most of the time, practicing the necessary techniques.

Topics

  • Basics of Unit testing
  • State-verification with the help of Stubs
  • Behaviour-verification with the help of Mocks
  • Parametrized tests
  • Refactoring your Tests

What you need to bring with you

  • Basic knowledge of an xUnit framework, which you'll have if you've wrote simple tests already
  • A laptop with a development environment installed that you want to work with during the course, such as Eclipse, IntelliJ, Visual Studio or even vim
